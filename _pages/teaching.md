I have served as a **Graduate Teaching Assistant** at **Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University (Virginia Tech)** since **August 2022**.

I support courses through a full teaching workflow: instruction, recitations, curriculum reinforcement, student mentoring, assessment, and LMS coordination.

**Instruction and Course Management**
- Facilitated undergraduate and graduate instruction for **CS 5704 (Software Engineering)**, **CS 5614 (Big Data Engineering)**, and **EE 204 (Computer Architecture)**.

**Curriculum Development and Instructional Design**
- Delivered recitations, guided technical discussions, and reinforced foundational and advanced concepts in software systems and data engineering.

**Student Mentoring and Academic Support**
- Conducted regular office hours, provided individualized mentoring, and supported student success through structured feedback and classroom management.

**Student Assessment**
- Designed grading rubrics, developed quizzes and exam components, and evaluated assignments to strengthen comprehension, problem-solving, and code quality.

**Learning Management Systems (LMS)**
- Managed LMS workflows for distributing materials, tracking progress, and coordinating instructional communication.
